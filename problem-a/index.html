Doraemon

Doraemon is a robotic cat from the 22nd century in the Japanese manga series called Doraemon. He comes to 20th century and stays with Nobita's family. Doraemon can pull all sorts of special devices from 22nd century out of his 4D pocket.

  • Always ready to help Nobita
  • Has a magical pocket with futuristic gadgets
  • Likes to eat dorayaki
